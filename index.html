About Me

As a seasoned Space Systems Engineer based in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, I've contributed to a diverse range of space missions. My expertise lies in the design, development, and integration of intricate embedded systems, resulting in the successful deployment of operational systems. With a proven track record in satellite AIT, software engineering, and space systems engineering, I'm well-equipped to tackle complex challenges in the aerospace industry.

Dedicated to fostering knowledge sharing and accessibility, I've curated a comprehensive collection of open-source resources on space systems engineering. Recognizing the scattered and even hidden nature of information in this field, I've consolidated these valuable materials into a centralized GitHub repository. This resource aims to empower both novice and seasoned engineers by providing a repo of previous work and accelerating their learning journey.
