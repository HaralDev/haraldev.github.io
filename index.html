About Me

Welcome, I am Harald Luiks, a Space Systems Assembly and Integration (AIT) engineer located in Delft, the Netherlands. With two years space missions experience on three space missions (see portfolio below), I have experience in developing and integrating complicated embedded systems to successful systems in operation. During my Master studies, I was also a part-time freelancer, working as software developer and researcher for various startups. My experience gives me a strong background in satellite AIT, software development and space systems engineering.

System AIT engineer at ISISPACE
2021 - present

At ISISPACE I am responsible for the system assembly, integration and testing (AIT) for cubesatellites for almost 2 years. On a high level, I am tasked with ensuring that system requirements are verified. This involves planning the AIT phase, preparing AIV/AIT documentation and procedures, maintaining the VCD, design and procurement of GSE, design and procurement of satellite harnessing. Moreover, the work includes extensive day-to-day logistics, problem solving, giving input on NRBs, performing investigations.
